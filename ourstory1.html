In the Beginning

When we started COFFEE ROASTERS, we dreamt of creating a place where locals would gather to share a pint, a cup of coffee, or a great meal. We called it the “Locals Living Room” because we wanted an environment that was warm and felt like home. We longed to create a space that sparked creativity and inspired memorable conversation.
Here at COFFEE ROASTERS, we believe:
-Engaging with others over a meal or tasty beverage is good for the soul.
-Collaborating with our neighbors is better than competing.
-Everyone has value and deserves a second chance!


Our Mission

Our mission statement is simple:
“SERVE PEOPLE, BLESS THE CITY AND IMPACT THE WORLD.”
We created our Love the Fort mural with “Bless the City” in mind. Just to give back and say thanks. The idea behind our mural is to express visually what so many of us locals think daily as we stroll through our wonderful city.
Love and Worth are truly what makes us all feel valuable, and that’s just the reason we created BREWED.


Expect More Than a Coffee

We’re not just passionate purveyors of coffee, but everything else that goes with a full and rewarding coffeehouse experience. We also offer a selection of premium teas, fine pastries and other delectable treats to please the taste buds.
And the music you hear in store is chosen for its artistry and appeal.
We make sure everything we do is through the lens of humanity – from our commitment to the highest quality coffee in the world, to the way we engage with our customers and communities to do business responsibly.
